Definitions:

  1. An individual is vaccinated if:
    • received the recommended number of doses of one of the three FDA authorized vaccines and
      • J&J: 1 dose
      • Pfizer-BioNTech: 2 dose
      • Moderna 2 doses
    • 14 days have elapsed since the last dose
  2. A family is vaccinated if
    • all of the adults over the age of 16 are vaccinated
